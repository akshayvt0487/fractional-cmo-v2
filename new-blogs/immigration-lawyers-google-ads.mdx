# Google Ads for Immigration Lawyers: Complete PPC Strategy Guide 2026

Google Ads is one of the most effective channels for immigration lawyers to reach clients actively searching for visa assistance. This guide covers strategy, implementation, and optimisation for maximum ROI.

## Why Google Ads Works for Immigration Lawyers

### Key Advantages

- **High-intent audience**: People searching for visa help need services now
- **Precise targeting**: Target by visa type, location, and intent
- **Measurable results**: Track every enquiry and cost
- **Scalable**: Increase budget as you prove ROI
- **Immediate visibility**: Start generating leads quickly

## Campaign Structure

Proper structure maximises relevance and quality scores.

### Recommended Campaign Structure

**Campaign 1: Skilled Migration**
- Ad Group: Skilled Visa Lawyer
- Ad Group: Points Test Migration
- Ad Group: Employer Sponsored Visa
- Ad Group: 482 Visa Agent

**Campaign 2: Partner & Family**
- Ad Group: Partner Visa Lawyer

**Campaign 5: Appeals & Reviews**
- Ad Group: Visa Refused Help
- Ad Group: AAT Appeal Lawyer
- Ad Group: Visa Cancellation Help

## Keyword Strategy

### High-Intent Keywords

**Service Keywords**
- immigration lawyer [city]
- migration agent near me
- visa lawyer Australia
- immigration solicitor [suburb]

**Visa-Specific Keywords**
- partner visa lawyer
- skilled migration agent
- student visa help
- business visa specialist
- [subclass number] visa help

**Problem Keywords**
- visa refused appeal
- visa cancelled help
- bridging visa lawyer
- immigration review tribunal

### Match Types

**Exact Match**: [partner visa lawyer sydney]
- Highest intent
- Best conversion rates
- Higher CPCs

**Phrase Match**: "immigration lawyer"
- Captures variations
- Good for discovery
- Moderate intent

### Negative Keywords

Essential negatives:
- jobs, careers, employment (unless targeting employers)
- free, pro bono
- DIY, self-application
- [competitor names] (usually)
- study, course (unless relevant)
- salary, pay

## Ad Copy Best Practices

### Compliance Requirements

- Display MARA registration number
- No guarantees of visa approval
- Accurate claims only
- Appropriate disclaimers

### Headline Formulas

**Headline 1**: Service + Location
"Immigration Lawyer Sydney"

**Headline 2**: Specialisation
"Partner Visa Specialists"

**Headline 3**: Trust + CTA
"MARA Registered | Free Consult"

### Description Writing

- Lead with client benefit
- Include credentials
- Address common concerns
- Clear call-to-action

**Example**:
"Expert partner visa assistance from MARA registered agents. 95% success rate on eligible cases. Free initial consultation. Call today for assessment."

### Ad Extensions

**Sitelinks**
- Partner Visas
- Skilled Migration
- Visa Appeals
- Free Assessment

**Callouts**
- MARA Registered
- 20+ Years Experience
- Free Consultation
- Multilingual Service

**Structured Snippets**
- Visa types: Partner, Skilled, Student, Business

## Landing Page Optimisation

### Essential Elements

**Above the Fold**
- Headline matching ad
- MARA credentials visible
- Simple enquiry form
- Phone number prominent
- Trust signals

**Supporting Content**
- Visa process overview
- Team credentials
- Client testimonials
- FAQ section
- Success indicators

### Conversion Optimisation

- Single, clear CTA
- Minimal form fields (name, email, phone, visa type)
- Mobile optimised
- Fast load time
- Trust signals throughout

### Visa-Specific Pages

Create dedicated landing pages for each visa category:
- Partner visa landing page
- Skilled migration landing page
- Student visa landing page
- Appeals landing page

## Bidding and Budget

### Bidding Strategies

**Starting Out: Manual CPC**
- Full control
- Learn keyword values
- Optimise based on data

**After 30+ Conversions: Target CPA**
- Algorithm optimisation
- Set target cost per enquiry
- Requires conversion history

### Budget Guidelines

**Starting Budget**: $3,000-5,000/month
- Test multiple campaigns
- Gather sufficient data
- Identify best performers

**Scaling**: Based on CPL and case value
- Increase winning campaigns
- Reduce underperformers
- Test new opportunities

## Geo-Targeting

### Location Strategy

**Primary Markets**
- Major cities (Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane)
- Highest bids
- Most budget

**Secondary Markets**
- Regional areas
- Moderate bids
- Test and expand

**International Targeting**
- Consider offshore applicants
- Time zone considerations
- Currency display settings

## Remarketing

### Audience Segments

- All website visitors

### Remarketing Strategy

- Address common hesitations
- Reinforce expertise
- Testimonial-focused ads
- Time-sensitive messaging

## Tracking and Measurement

### Conversion Tracking

**Primary Conversions**
- Form submissions
- Phone calls (tracked)
- Chat initiations

**Secondary Conversions**
- Resource downloads
- Page engagement
- Time on site

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Click-through rate | 4-7% |
| Cost per click | $10-35 |
| Conversion rate | 5-10% |
| Cost per enquiry | $50-120 |
| Quality score | 7+ |

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Broad match without negatives**: Wastes budget
2. **Generic landing pages**: Hurts conversions
3. **Missing compliance elements**: Risk of issues
4. **Ignoring mobile**: Most searches are mobile
5. **No call tracking**: Missing conversions
6. **Single ad per group**: Can't optimise
