# Google Ads for Finance Brokers: Complete PPC Strategy Guide 2026

Google Ads is one of the most effective channels for finance brokers to generate qualified leads. This comprehensive guide covers campaign strategy, keyword research, ad optimisation, and performance tracking.

## Why Google Ads Works for Finance Brokers

Google Ads captures high-intent prospects actively searching for lending solutions.

### Key Advantages

- **Intent-based targeting**: Reach people actively searching for loans
- **Geographic precision**: Target specific service areas
- **Measurable results**: Track every dollar spent
- **Scalable**: Increase budget as results improve
- **Fast results**: Generate leads from day one

## Campaign Structure for Finance Brokers

Proper campaign structure maximises relevance and quality scores.

### Recommended Structure

**Campaign 1: Home Loans**
- Ad Group: Mortgage Broker [City]
- Ad Group: Home Loan Broker
- Ad Group: First Home Buyer Loan

**Campaign 2: Investment Loans**
- Ad Group: Investment Property Loan
- Ad Group: Investment Mortgage Broker
- Ad Group: Property Investor Finance

**Campaign 3: Refinancing**
- Ad Group: Refinance Home Loan
- Ad Group: Mortgage Refinance
- Ad Group: Better Home Loan Rate

**Campaign 4: Commercial/Business**
- Ad Group: Commercial Finance Broker
- Ad Group: Business Loan Broker
- Ad Group: Commercial Property Finance

## Keyword Strategy

### High-Intent Keywords

**Service Keywords**
- mortgage broker [city]
- home loan broker near me
- finance broker [suburb]
- best mortgage broker [location]

**Loan Type Keywords**
- investment loan broker
- first home buyer loan
- refinance mortgage
- construction loan

**Urgency Keywords**
- home loan pre-approval
- fast mortgage approval
- quick home loan
- urgent refinance

### Keyword Match Types

**Exact Match**: [mortgage broker sydney]
- Highest intent, lowest volume
- Best conversion rates
- Premium CPCs

**Phrase Match**: "mortgage broker"
- Moderate intent and volume
- Good for discovering variations
- Balanced CPCs

**Negative Keywords**
Essential negatives include:
- jobs, careers, salary
- training, course, certification
- free, DIY
- complaints, reviews (unless targeting)
- competitor names (usually)

## Ad Copy Best Practices

### Compliance Requirements

- Include ACL number in ad or landing page
- No guarantees of approval
- Accurate claims only
- Appropriate disclaimers

### Headline Formulas

**Headline 1**: Service + Location
"Mortgage Broker Sydney"

**Headline 2**: Value Proposition
"Compare 40+ Lenders"

**Headline 3**: Social Proof/CTA
"500+ Happy Clients | Free Consult"

### Description Best Practices

- Lead with main benefit
- Include trust signals
- Clear call-to-action
- Differentiation from competitors

### Ad Extensions

**Sitelink Extensions**
- Home Loans
- Investment Loans
- Refinancing
- Free Consultation

**Callout Extensions**
- Free Service
- 40+ Lenders
- Same Day Pre-Approval
- Local Expert

**Call Extensions**
- Display phone number
- Track calls as conversions
- Set business hours

## Landing Page Optimisation

### Essential Elements

**Above the Fold**
- Clear headline matching ad
- Value proposition
- Trust signals (ACL, logos)
- Simple contact form
- Phone number

**Supporting Content**
- Service explanation
- Process overview
- Testimonials
- FAQs
- Lender panel

### Conversion Optimisation

- Single clear CTA
- Minimal form fields
- Mobile optimised
- Fast load time (<3 seconds)
- Chat option

## Bidding and Budget Strategy

### Bidding Strategies

**Manual CPC**: Best for starting out
- Full control over bids
- Learn keyword values
- Optimise based on data

**Target CPA**: After 30+ conversions
- Algorithm optimisation
- Set target cost per lead
- Requires conversion history

### Budget Allocation

**Starting Budget**: $2,000-4,000/month
- Test multiple campaigns
- Gather data quickly
- Identify winners

**Scaling Budget**: Based on CPL
- Increase budget on profitable campaigns
- Pause underperformers
- Test new keywords

## Geo-Targeting

### Location Strategy

**Primary Area**
- Your main service region
- Highest bids
- Most budget

**Secondary Areas**
- Expansion targets
- Moderate bids
- Test performance

**Exclusions**
- Areas you don't service
- Low-value regions
- Competitor-heavy areas

## Remarketing for Finance Brokers

### Remarketing Ad Strategy

- Address common objections
- Highlight unique benefits

## Conversion Tracking

### What to Track

- Form submissions
- Phone calls (tracked)
- Chat initiations
- Calculator completions
- Page engagement

### Attribution Setup

- Primary conversions: Forms and calls
- Secondary conversions: Calculator, engagement
- Conversion window: 30 days
- Attribution model: Data-driven or position-based

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Broad match without negatives**: Wastes budget on irrelevant searches
2. **Single ad per group**: Can't test and optimise
3. **Generic landing pages**: Hurts quality score and conversions
4. **Ignoring mobile**: Majority of searches are mobile
5. **No call tracking**: Missing significant conversion data
6. **Compliance violations**: Risk of account suspension
